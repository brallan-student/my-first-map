## What did I build? 
["My 2025 summer review" App](https://brallan-student.github.io/my-first-map/)  is a one-page mobile-friendly web app that shows my top three favorite internships of the summer of 2025. there are picture aswell that shows a day of that internship.

The audience are my classmates, as well the teachers whpo work at the school and are intrested in looking further in my class of Digital arts. 


## Tech Stack 

1. [Google My Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/), for generating the location of my internships.
2. [Mapbox GL JS](https://docs.mapbox.com/mapbox-gl-js/mapstyle)library, for styling and displaying maps, and adding camera behaviors (flyto animations).
3. [Visual Studio Code](https://code.visualstudio.com/download) free IDE, with [Live Server](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=ritwickdey.LiveServer) and [Markdown All in One](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=yzhang.markdown-all-in-one) extensions.
4. [GitHub pages](https://docs.github.com/en/pages/getting-started-with-github-pages/creating-a-github-pages-site), for publishing the app for free! so people can explore my map.
